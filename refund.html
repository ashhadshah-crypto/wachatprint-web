Refund Policy

Effective date: April 13, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds are handled for WAChatPrint subscriptions and services.

1. Subscription Charges

WAChatPrint may offer paid subscription plans for higher usage limits, larger file sizes, and additional features.

2. General Refund Policy

Payments are generally non-refundable once charged, except where required by law or where WAChatPrint determines, at its sole discretion, that a refund is appropriate.

3. Duplicate or Incorrect Charges

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, charged more than once, or experienced a billing error, contact us and we will review the issue.

4. Service Issues

If you experience a material service issue and believe a refund should be considered, contact us with full details including your account email, payment date, and issue description.

5. Cancellation

Canceling a subscription stops future billing but does not automatically create a refund for charges already paid, unless required by law or approved by WAChatPrint.

6. Chargebacks and Disputes

Before filing a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider, please contact us first so we can review and try to resolve the issue directly.

7. Contact

For billing and refund questions, contact: hello@wachatprint.com

This is a starter refund policy for your MVP and should be reviewed by legal counsel before wider public launch.
