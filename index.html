Frequently asked questions

These are the most important things users typically want to know before using the service.

What file formats do you support?

WAChatPrint currently supports WhatsApp chat exports in TXT and ZIP format.

Does ZIP support include media?

For now, ZIP support extracts the chat TXT file inside the archive. Media rendering can be added later.

Are my files private?

User accounts and saved items are private. Generated PDFs stored in history use protected access and signed downloads.

Can I download old PDFs again?

Yes. Saved items in your dashboard history can be downloaded again as long as they were stored successfully.

Why are some large PDFs not saved?

Very large generated PDFs may download successfully but may not be saved in history due to storage size limits on the current plan.

Is this an official WhatsApp product?

No. WAChatPrint is an independent tool and is not affiliated with WhatsApp or Meta.
